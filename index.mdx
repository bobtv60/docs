## Welcome to UpView

UpView is the ultimate feedback collection and bug tracking platform designed specifically for Roblox game developers. Collect player feedback, track bugs, and gain insights to improve your games with our powerful dashboard and easy-to-use API.

  <Card title="Analyze Trends" icon="trending-up">
